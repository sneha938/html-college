Samskruthi – Intercollegiate Management and Cultural Fest

A two day inter-collegiate fest Samskruthi 2020 started with big pomp and show on 25th Feb 2020. The fest is jointly organised by BMS College of Commerce and Management, V V Puram, Bengaluru and BMS Evening College of Arts and Commerce, Basavanagudi, Bengaluru on 25th & 26th February 2020. The fest is dedicated to “FIT INDIA MOVEMENT – A STEP TOWARDS HEALTHY INDIA”. To support the cause Zumba and fitness class was organised for students from 7-8 AM in the morning. The intercollegiate fest Samskruthi 2020 was inaugurated on 25th Feb 2020 by Mr. Ramesh Arvind, Indian Film Actor/Director, Miss Shanvi Srivatsava, Indian film actress & Mr. Vijay Raghavendra, Indian Film Actor/Director. The Guests of Honor were - Dr. B.S Ragini Narayan, Donor Trustee, BMS Educational Trust, Sri. M. Madan Gopal, IAS(Retd.) Trustee BMSET, Sri Aviram Sharma, Chairman, BMSCCM and Prof. Pankaj Choudhary, Principal, BMSCCM & BMSECAC. The evening program also witnessed an amazing violin performance by Wireless Walking Violinist– Dr. Aneesh Vidyashankar followed by DJ Night in the college ground. On 26th Feb 2020, the program was concluded with valedictory program and spine-chilling live concert by the famous Kannada International Rock Band formed by KFI, Naik and Jois Project. The winners of different events organised at college are as follows: Fashion show- IIFA Lancester Degree College Mad ads- Sindhi College Bulls and bears mock stock- St. Paul college Skyscraper - SBMJEC Radio jockeying - MLA college The voice- Vijaya college Dumb charades- Karunya first grade college Face painting - Florence college Debate - PES college Best manager - PES college Start up 100 - Transcend College Product launch- PES college Quiz – Surabhi P & Vignesh from Transend College

The college organises an orientation program for all the newly joined students of the college in the beginning of the session.
