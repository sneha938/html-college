FACILITIES

FACILITIES

CLASS ROOMS

Spacious, Air-Conditioned classrooms are provided for excellent teaching and learning environment. The students enjoy their classes in the modern furniture with a mix of old antique wooden benches which are more than 60 years old. All the classrooms are equipped with smart board/LCD projectors and modern teaching aids. This makes the teaching and learning in the classroom with PPT presentations and cases studies discussion very comfortable..

COMPUTER LAB

Computer knowledge is the most essential requirement of present legal education. For this purpose, our computer lab is artistically and ergonomically designed, where students get an opportunity to develop their skills according to the global needs. One of the computer lab is designed keeping in mind the modern corporate environment and the 2nd lab has been designed to give students international exposure to major software

SEMINAR HALL

The campus has a posh multipurpose seminar hall with all the latest audio, video and public address systems. All functions, Guest Lectures, Seminars, Conferences, and Symposiums are conducted in the auditorium. The Seminar Hall is also used as multipurpose hall for many students activities like theatre practice, Dance Practice, Yoga and many more recreational activities from time to time.

LIBRARY

Library is the place of knowledge open for academicians and students. The library of the college has good collection of Commerce, General Management, Marketing, Human Resource, Finance, Accounting, Project Management, Business Statistics, Language etc. The library at BMSCCM houses renowned Indian and International Journals. The E-Library facility with Kindle and Inflibnet gives our students to access millions of books online. Our library provides Inter Library Loan facility with sister Institutions of BMS Educational Trust, News Paper Clipping service etc. The other facilities available at the library include reading room, computer lab with internet facility, uninterrupted power supply and backup facility.
